What To Bring

  • A Bible (if you have one)
  • Good attitude
  • Sleeping bag (or sheet/blanket set)
  • Pillow
  • Sun screen
  • Bug repellent
  • Flashlight
  • Swimsuit (modest)
  • Swim Goggles
  • Towels
  • Shirts, shorts, long pants
  • A set of clothes that can get dirty for messy games
  • Underwear, socks
  • Jacket, hat
  • Sweater
  • Raincoat
  • Running shoes, sandals
  • Soap, shampoo
  • Toothbrush, toothpaste
  • Canteen money (if not prepaid)
  • Water bottle

You will be notified if a specific item (e.g.-white t shirt for tie-dyeing) is requested.

What Not To Bring 

  • Bad attitudes
  • Bad behaviour
  • Communicable diseases
  • Weapons and replicas
  • Knives/matches/lighters/fireworks
  • Guns (including water, airsoft, paint ball)
  • Pets
  • Cigarettes
  • Alcoholic beverages
  • Illegal drugs
  • Cell phones
  • Electronic devices (mp3 players, laptops, iPods, PSP, etc)
    • due to damage, theft or loss and because you are going to be having too much fun to pay attention to these things:-)
  • Peanuts/nut products/snacks
    • despite our best efforts of not serving peanuts/nuts or peanut/nut products, we cannot guarantee that our facilities will be peanut-free or nut-free.
    • Please help us reduce the risk by not bringing peanuts/nuts or peanut/nut products to our facility.
