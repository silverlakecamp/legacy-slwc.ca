Here are some incentives that are made to help you save money on your camp registration.

  • Some exceptions apply.
  • Choose which incentive will be to your best advantage.

Bring 3 - Come Free!

  • Invite three friends who have not been to a week long camp at SLWC in the last three years.
  • They pay and go to the same or other SLWC camp as you.
  • You pay full registration fee and attend the same or another SLWC camp as your friend(s).
  • When all have paid and attended you will be refunded;

 

  • 25% for 1 first time camper!
  • 50% for 2 first time campers!!
  • 100% for 3 first time campers!!!

 

  • (This incentive cannot be combined with any other offer, incentive or discount)
  • (This offer does not apply to Collide, LIT Camp, Family Camp, or Geezer Camp)

 

 

Returning Camper Discount

  • If you have already been to a SLWC children's camp you are entitled to a discount from your registration fee. Simply select 'returning camper' when registering!
  • You may combine this  with Early Registration and Multiple Child Discounts only.

 

  • (This offer does not apply to Collide, Family Camp, LIT Camp, or Geezer Camp)

 

Early Bird Discount

  • Register before April 1st, 2018
  • (Registration fee to be paid in full by the early registration date in order to receive this discount)

 

  • (With the exception of the Returning Camper Discount this discount cannot be combined with any other offer, incentive or discount)
  • (This offer does not apply to Collide, Family Camp, LIT Camp, or Geezer Camp)

 

Multiple Sibling Discount

  • 2nd Sibling $35.00 off
  • 3rd Sibling $55.00 off
  • 4th Sibling and up FREE!

 

  • (With the exception of the Returning Camper Discount this incentive cannot be combined with any other offer, incentice or discount)
  • (This offer does not apply to all camps)

 

Second Camp 1/2 Price

  • Register for a second week long camp in your age bracket and come for 1/2 the regular registration cost.

 

  • (This incentive cannot be combined with any other offer, incentive or discount)
  • (This offer does not apply to Collide, Family Camp, or Geezer Camp)
