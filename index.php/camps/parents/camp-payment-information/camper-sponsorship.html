 Because we know  it is difficult for some families to get their kids to camp, we want to help. Here is how it works: 

  • We find people/churches or other sources of funding to create a "Sponsorship Fund".
  • We assume it would cost something for you to keep your child at home during the week they are coming to camp and ask you to share part of the registration cost, if at all possible.
  • You make application for sponsorship funding.
  • We request two non-family references who support you in your application process.
  • We review your application and respond.

We want every child to have a camping experience if they so desire, so we do all we can to make that possible. If money is getting in your way, please complete the application form below so your children can enjoy one of the best experiences that life has to offer. You may also contact the office if you wish to speak to someone in person. Call 1-877-511-CAMP (2267) toll free. 

Please be assured that we do not share your information with the public and respect your right to privacy in this application. 

Let us do you good...we really want to!
