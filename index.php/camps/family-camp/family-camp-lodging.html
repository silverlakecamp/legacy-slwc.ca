 Accomodations

 

1.  Tenting Sites: Brook Kidron Tenting Sites
Fees: $25 per night* or $135.00 for 6 nights
Amenities: Water and nearby comfort station (washroom and shower)

 

2.  Trailer Sites: Brook Kidron Trailer Sites
Fees: $35 per night* or $189.00 for 6 nights for 15 Amp Service
Fees: $40 per night* or $216.00 for 6 nights for 30 Amp Service
Amenities: Water and nearby comfort station (washroom and shower), 
Lot Size: For 30' to 36' trailers
Note: For longer stays refer below to section below called "Leaseholder Lots"

 

3.  Cabins: Cedarholm Cabins (Price is for up to 5 guests)
Fees: $40 per night* or $216.00 for 6 nights
Amenities: heat, and nearby comfort station (washroom and shower)
Sleeps: 3 Cabins - sleeps 4; 1 Cabin - sleeps 3
Additional cot may be available upon request
Additional person fee: $5 per night or $27.00 for 6 nights
 

4.  Cottage: Hill Top Villa (Price is for up to 9 guests)
Fees: $110.00 per night* or $594.00 for 6 nights
Amenities: 1 full & 1 1/2 bathroom, full kitchen (stove & fridge, etc.) heat, bedding & towels provided
Sleeps: Total 9: 2 upper Rooms -1 room sleeps 2 (has 1 queen) & 1 room sleeps 4 (has 2 queens); 1 Ground Level room -sleeps 3 (has 1 dbl +1 single)
Addition person fee: $5 per night or $27.00 for 6 nights

5.  Motel Room: Bethany Lodge (Price is for up to 4 guests)
Fees: $65.00 per night* or $351.00 for 6 nights  $70.00 per night* $378.00 for 6 nights

Amenities: full bathroom, AC & heat, bedding & towels provided
Sleeps: 6 Rooms sleep 4 (have 2 dbl); 6 room sleeps 4 (has 1 king +1 bunk)
Additional person fee: $5 per person per night or $27.00 for 6 nights

6.  Administration Building Room: (Prices are for up to 4 guests)
Fees: $40.00 per night* or $216.00 for 6 nights
Amenities: shared full bathroom, Bedding & Towels provided
Sleeps: 2 Rooms - sleep 2 (1 dbl); 1 room - sleeps 4 (1 dbl +1 bunk); 1 room - sleeps 3 (1 dbl + 1 single)
Additional person fee: $5 per person per night or $27.00 for 6 nights 

7.  Dorm Room: Silverholm (Prices are for up to 5 guests in the larger rooms, and up to 3 guests in the smaller rooms)
Fees: $30.00/$20.00 per night* or $162.00/$108.00 for 6 nights
Amenities: nearby comfort station (washroom and shower)
Sleeps: 8 Rooms - sleeps 10 (have 5 bunks); 4 Rooms - sleeps 4 (2 bunks)
Additional person fee: $5.00 per person per night or $27.00 for 6 nights  

8.  Dorm Room: Boys Dormitory (Prices are for up to 5 guests)   
Fees: $25.00 per night* or $135.00 for 6 nights
Amenities: nearby comfort station (washroom and shower)
Sleeps: 2 rooms - sleep 10 (5 bunks), 2 rooms - sleep 8 (4 bunks), 3 rooms - sleep 9 (4 bunks and one single) 
 Additional person fee: $5 per person per night or $27.00 for 6 nights

 

*Additional Discounts  
The above prices listed for our accomodations for single night stays, are discounted as follows if you stay for 3 or more nights.
     
3 or 4 night stay 5%  
5 or 6 night stay 10%  
     

 

Leaseholder Lots

  1. Trailer Lots: Brook Kidron Trailer Lots
    • Family Camp lodging Fee: $0.00
    • Fees (2017 rate): $780.00 per season or $935.00 for annual
    • Fees (2018 rate): $794.00 per season or $953.00 for annual
    • Note: Additional Rules & Conditions apply; 5% discount if paid two months prior to anniversary date
    • Seasonal: May Victoria Weekend to Sunday Thanksgiving Weekend (with trailer removal)
    • Annual: 365 days (with trailer onsite)
    • Amenities: Water and nearby comfort station (washroom and shower), some 15 amp and some 30 Amp 
    • Lot Size: for 30' to 36' trailers

     

  2. Cottages : 
    • Family Camp lodging Fee: $0.00
    • Fees: private sale arrangements
    • Note: Additional Rules & Conditions apply
