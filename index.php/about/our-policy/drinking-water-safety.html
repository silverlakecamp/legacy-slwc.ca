You are here: About » Our Policy » Drinking Water Safety

SLWC holds to a very strict drinking water treatment, monitoring and testing regimine which ensures a safe drinking water supply for all our campers and guests.

The following information and documents are available at the SLWC office for review by the public free of charge during normal business hours.

 

Drinking-Water Systems 

1. All test results

2. Annual Reports (The 2016/2017 annual water systems report is now available.  If you would like a copy of this report, please contact the camp office at office@slwc.ca)

3. Trained Person certificates

4. EER and Declaration of Professional Engineer 

5. Copy of O. Reg. 170/3
