A very dedicated, committed group voluntarily serves SLWC as the Board of Directors. Generous with their time, knowledge and energy.   They continually strive to ensure the integrity and forward movement of the camp.

 

About our Board

We are blessed to have many volunteers to help us with our Camp! Our board members are approved by the DBA or DIstrict Conference, and serve up to an initial 3 year term (can be extended). Our new board members start their term in September of each year.

Our Board Positions

Board Chair: Bernie Fast - Appointed by the District Conference (2006-present)

  • DBA Representative: Rev. Bert McCutcheon (2014-present)
  • Secretary Representative: John Ryan (2015-present Recording Secretary)
  • Treasurer: Ross Chapman (2016-present)
  • Members at Large: Jennifer Burman (2017), Karen Gilks, Rob McIntyre (2008), and Jim Wilkinson
