Volunteer Opportunities

Last year, over 38,000 hours of work were done at Silver Lake in the process of Ministering to our children, youth, and others.  Much of that work was done by volunteers.  We covet, value, and depend on the work of our volunteers.  We also recognize that it is exciting to put all of our efforts together, for the cause of making and shaping Disciples of Christ.

Below, you will find a list of activities we need help with completing during 2018.  If you are able to commit to complete one of these tasks, simply email the office specifying which task you are able to help with!  We will respond to you directly to confirm that your offer has been accepted, or in the case that someone else has already been assigned that task, that your help would not be needed.

As a supporter of our ministries here at Silver Lake, we are trusting you will find a place to plug in to and use your gifts.

Please feel free to contact us and ask about opportunities to help as well - there is often short term needs and many that aren't listed below!

 

Hospitality (Food services and or help with Accommodations)

Summer Retreats

Property Management (Maintenance, grounds keeping etc.)

  • Splitting Firewood
  • Basic Plumbing Projects
  • Small Building Projects
  • General Yard Work/Clean up
  • Painting (outdoor & indoor)
  • Gardening (Spring - Fall Season) - We have many flower beds and gardens that need watering and TLC. This is an area we are always looking for help in, and would welcome help at any point throughout the spring-summer season. Please contact the office below if you are interested and available to help!

 

Business management (Office work, financial work and Human Resourcing)

Ministry (Teaching, preaching, counselling etc.)

If you are interested in any of these opportunities please email the office specifying which of the above opportunities you would be available to assist in!
